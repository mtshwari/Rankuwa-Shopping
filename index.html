About This Project

This project was inspired by my passion for simplifying technology and making it accessible to everyone. The idea came to life after realizing how challenging it can be for people to find the right technology products without feeling overwhelmed. My goal was to create a user-friendly platform where individuals can explore, compare, and purchase the best products that suit their needs.

I started brainstorming ideas in early 2024, and after several iterations and feedback sessions, this platform was developed. It is a portfolio project for Holberton School, showcasing my ability to build robust, dynamic web applications independently.
